## What is Typing Club Bot?

Typing Club Bot is a JavaScript snippet you paste into your browser's developer console on [typingclub.com](https://www.typingclub.com). Once running, it:

1. Reads every character in the current lesson from the DOM
2. Sends each keystroke to TypingClub's internal API at the correct timing
3. Navigates to the next level automatically after each lesson completes
4. Repeats until all levels are finished

No installation, no browser extension, no account required — just open the console, paste, and let it run.

<Note>
Keep your laptop plugged in and the browser tab active while the bot runs. TypingClub requires the page to remain focused to register keystrokes correctly.
</Note>
